About KBI Netherlands

KBI Netherlands is the Netherlands chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute. It is a non-profit organisation focused on emerging-technology education, competitions, and community building across Europe.

Key Facts
  • Netherlands chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute
  • 100+ community members across the Netherlands and Europe
  • 3 core technology tracks: Blockchain, Data Science, Quantum
  • 7+ years of programmes and events delivered
