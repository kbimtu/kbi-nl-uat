ETO Western 2026 Coming to InHolland University of Applied Sciences

8–9 October 2026 — KBI's flagship event in the Netherlands returns to InHolland, open to participants worldwide.

2026

KBI Netherlands is pleased to confirm that ETO Western 2026 will take place on 8–9 October at InHolland University of Applied Sciences, Amsterdam.

ETO Western 2026 is an international showcase event, open to participants from around the world. It brings together outstanding student and graduate work across three emerging-technology domains:

  • BCTT — Blockchain, Cryptography, and Trust Technologies
  • DSAI — Data Science and Artificial Intelligence
  • QCIT — Quantum Computing and Information Theory

The event is not a closed regional finals — it is a worldwide invitation to present serious applied work in these fields. Participants from any country are welcome.

InHolland has been a core partner of KBI Netherlands since the chapter's earliest years, hosting international competition events since 2023. ETO Western 2026 continues that relationship as the chapter's most significant event of the year.

Registration and participation details will be announced shortly. To register interest, contact us via the contact page.
