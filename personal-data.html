Personal Data Statement

Persoonsgegevens Verklaring

Last updated: June 2026

This statement details categories of personal data processed by KBI and how we protect them.

Categories of Data

  • Identity data: name, date of birth, nationality (where required for events)
  • Contact data: email, phone, address, organisation
  • Programme data: submissions, scores, certificates, membership status
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, cookie identifiers

Security Measures

We implement access controls, encryption in transit, and staff training. Data breaches are reported to authorities within 72 hours where required.

Third Parties

We share data with hosting providers, email services, and event partners only as necessary, under data processing agreements.
