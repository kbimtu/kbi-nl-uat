Who We Are

About KBI Netherlands

KBI Netherlands is part of the Königsberger Bridges Institute (KBI) — an international nonprofit named after the Königsberg Bridges Problem, the mathematical puzzle that Euler solved in 1736 by inventing graph theory. We have been applying that same principle to students ever since: structured thinking leads to real outcomes.

We bring together students, educators, researchers, and industry in the Netherlands to do what most institutions do not: connect rigorous thinking with real-world application, from day one.

Who We Serve

Where do you fit?

Whether you are a student ready to compete, an educator looking to bring KBI to your institution, or an organisation that wants to partner — there is a clear entry point for you.

For Students

You do not need to be a coder. You need to be curious, collaborative, and willing to tackle real problems.

KBI Netherlands gives students structured, credible pathways into emerging technologies — whether you are a secondary school student just starting out or a university student ready to compete internationally.

International competition

Enter IBCOL, IDSOL, IQCOL, and ETO Western through the I2OL scheme.

Certification and training

Earn recognised credentials in blockchain, data science, and quantum computing.

Mentorship and community

Connect with peers, alumni, and industry professionals across the KBI network.

Career pathways

Competition records and KBI recognition strengthen university applications and CVs.

For Educators & Institutions

Give your students access to something the syllabus cannot.

KBI Netherlands extends learning beyond the classroom — connecting your students to international competition stages, hands-on training, and a community of peers and mentors across the Netherlands and beyond.

Curriculum-adjacent programmes

Competitions and training that complement STEM, computer science, and business curricula.

Institutional partnerships

Host I2OL regional rounds, co-brand events, and connect with KBI’s global university network.

Educator development

Workshops and community access for educators entering blockchain, data, and quantum fields.

Student achievement recognition

Official KBI certificates and competition results for programme records and reporting.

For Professionals

Give back. Find talent. Shape the field.

You know what the industry actually needs. We have the students who want to learn it. Whether you mentor, judge, speak, or collaborate — the community benefits, and so do you.

Mentorship

Mentor competition participants and training cohorts in blockchain, data science, or quantum computing.

Speaking & judging

Contribute as a guest speaker, panel judge at ETO Western, or workshop facilitator.

Research collaboration

Collaborate with KBI working groups on applied technology projects bridging academia and industry.

Talent pipeline

Early access to competition finalists and certification graduates for recruitment opportunities.

For Sponsors & Partners

Support the Netherlands’ next generation of technology leaders.

Your sponsorship funds serious, rigorous programmes that directly develop emerging-tech talent — with brand visibility, talent access, and mission alignment.

Brand visibility

Logo placement, event recognition, and programme visibility across KBI Netherlands channels.

Direct talent access

Introductions to finalists and certification graduates for internship and graduate recruitment.

CSR alignment

Support education and sustainable innovation aligned with your organisation’s CSR goals.

Flexible tiers

Bronze, Silver, Gold, and Platinum sponsorship tiers — or custom in-kind partnerships.
