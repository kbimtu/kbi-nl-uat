About ETO Western

Showcase. Celebrate. Connect.

ETO Western 2026 is not an olympiad finals event. It showcases and celebrates outstanding student work across three technology domains — BCTT (Blockchain, Cryptography and Trust Technologies), DSAI (Data Science and Artificial Intelligence), and QCIT (Quantum Computing and Information Theory).

The event is open to participants worldwide — not limited to any single region or conference. It brings together students, educators, and industry in the Netherlands for rigorous presentation, feedback, and community building aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals.
