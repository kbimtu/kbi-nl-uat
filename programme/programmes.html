Participant Pathway

How it fits together

Most members move through KBI Netherlands in roughly this order — though there is no strict requirement to follow every step.

01
Join & Train
Begin with SkillSprint, ETTI, or ABC5 to build technical foundations in one or more domains.
02
Compete
Enter IBCOL, IDSOL, or IQCOL through the I2OL scheme. Top performers represent the Netherlands at international finals.
03
Get Certified
Demonstrate competence through ISFRETIC — KBI's independent certification framework, separate from training.
04
Go International
Outstanding performers may be invited to ETO Western at InHolland or to ETO global events and partner competitions worldwide.
