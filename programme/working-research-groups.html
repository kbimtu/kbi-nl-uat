Working & Research Groups

Deeper inquiry beyond competition cycles — sustained communities of students, academics, and practitioners exploring emerging-technology frontiers.

Communities

Communities of inquiry and practice

Working & Research Groups provide space for sustained exploration — beyond the intensity of olympiad weekends or short training cohorts. Groups may start informally and formalise over time as outputs and membership grow.

Group Types

Different kinds of groups

Working Groups

Applied projects with defined deliverables and regular check-ins.

Research Groups

Longer-horizon inquiry with academic or industry publication goals.

Industry Labs

Partner-hosted exploration of regulated-industry use cases.

Infrastructure

Shared tooling, standards, and framework development — including ISFRETIC alignment.

Focus Areas

Current and emerging areas

Groups across the KBI network explore areas including AI safety, generative AI governance, self-sovereign identity, cybersecurity, quantum computing, digital economics, and SDG-aligned innovation — with the Netherlands chapter contributing EU regulatory and applied-sciences perspectives.
