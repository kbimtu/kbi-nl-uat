About KBI Netherlands

We connect theory to practice through applied STEM in the Netherlands.

KBI Netherlands is the Netherlands chapter of the Königsberger Bridges Institute — running olympiads, certification pathways, and community programmes for students and practitioners in blockchain, data science, and quantum computing. No hype. Just serious work.

Our Origin

Where We Come From

In 1736, Euler proved it was impossible to cross all seven bridges of Königsberg without repeating one — and in doing so invented graph theory, a foundation of modern networking, cryptography, and computing.

We took his name because we share his instinct: start with a hard question, work from first principles, and build something that actually holds. That is still how we design every programme at InHolland and across our Netherlands network.

Our Work

What We Do

We open doors in emerging technology — and make sure the people who walk through them are ready.

Compete

IBCOL, IDSOL, and IQCOL olympiads plus ETO Western — showcasing outstanding work in BCTT, DSAI, and QCIT.

Certify

ETTI, SkillSprint, ABC5, and ISFRETIC pathways for students who want depth, not just exposure.

Collab

Project support and working & research groups tackling SDG-aligned challenges with real outputs.

Connect

Socratic Circles, KBI Community Gatherings, and pathways to universities and industry across Europe.

Our Values

How We Work

These standards shape how we design programmes, run competitions, recognise results, and build partnerships across the Netherlands chapter.

Rigour

We do not simplify until we have understood the hard version first. Rigour is respect for the student, not gatekeeping.

Integrity

We say what we mean — including when the answer is “we do not know.” No overselling, no credential inflation.

Practicality

We build for the real world. Application over abstraction, always — from InHolland labs to regulated industries.
