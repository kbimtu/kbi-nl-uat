KBI Netherlands is not a content platform. Our programmes are structured, demanding, and slow — by design. When we talk about impact, we mean whether a student learned to think more carefully about a complex technology, and whether what they built here became the seed of something they carried into a degree, a lab, or a career.

Programme Impact

Three Tracks. One Common Standard.

Across blockchain, data science, and quantum computing, KBI Netherlands runs structured competitions and training programmes that ask students to do something difficult in front of people who know the subject.

Blockchain · IBCOL

Thinking About Blockchain Without the Hype

IBCOL challenges teams to design blockchain-based systems that could actually work — not just sound interesting. Judges are practitioners who care about architecture, feasibility, and ethics.

IBCOL details →
Data Science · IDSOL

Learning to Work With Data That Doesn’t Behave

IDSOL gives teams real, noisy datasets and asks them to find something honest and useful — under time pressure, with rigour and intellectual honesty.

IDSOL details →
Quantum · IQCOL

Early Exposure to Frontier Science

IQCOL invites students into one of the most technically demanding subjects in contemporary science — then explain their work to judges who will not pretend to understand something they do not.

SDG Alignment

Real-World Challenges

Every KBI programme encourages participants to address challenges aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals — from health and education to climate action and responsible innovation. KBI Netherlands programmes have produced award-winning solutions in healthcare, sustainability, finance, and education.

Healthcare

Health-tech proposals combining AI, data science, and regulated deployment pathways across EU markets.

Sustainability

Blockchain and data solutions for supply-chain transparency, carbon tracking, and circular economy models.

Education

Training pathways from competition to certification — building sustained learning communities beyond one-off events.
