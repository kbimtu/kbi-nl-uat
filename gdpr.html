GDPR Statement

AVG Verklaring

Last updated: June 2026

This statement describes how KBI complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (AVG/GDPR).

Lawful Basis

We process personal data based on consent, contractual necessity, legitimate interests, and legal obligations as applicable.

Data Retention

Registration and programme data is retained for the duration of the relationship plus statutory retention periods, typically up to seven years for financial records.

International Transfers

As a global organisation, data may be processed in the EEA and countries with adequacy decisions or appropriate safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses).

Data Protection Officer

Enquiries: privacy@kb.institute

Supervisory Authority

You may lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority. In Estonia: Andmekaitse Inspektsioon (AKI).
